Dare to Let Go

Fear is only the body remembering its limits.

Vanessa looked to her left and saw that where the triangular observation deck angled off, a woman and a man were standing there.

It was Lady Eliza and Sebastian Drake. He was looking out into the skyline.

Lady Eliza was standing close to him while looking towards Vanessa, she had a smile on her face.

“It is time to fly my dear! To transform yourself, say Mashig Kena Saltu Mataye Aasra Jameez Ebuta to return to your human form, think the words Chagaal Nemsaruth Basyen Mentora Yassmi” Sebastian said and smiled warmly at Vanessa.

“Do not resist me...” he whispered “...this is only the beginning!”

Vanessa realized then— some offers are not meant to be survived.
