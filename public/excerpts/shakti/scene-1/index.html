The Awakening

Power does not begin—it is remembered.

The ocean did not rage.

It shifted.

Beneath its surface, something ancient stirred— not summoned, not created— but released.

When she emerged, the world did not recognize her.

But it responded.

Storms bent.

Currents whispered.

And somewhere far beyond sight— something divine took notice.

Shakti was encircling the females.

Shakti the metamorphosed essence of Trishna, created by the force of the ocean.

Shakti the powerful rebirth of Desire, waiting deep inside the ocean,

for something to bring it upon land, something holy and unholy, someone who had earthly form,

Someone who was also a Girl.
