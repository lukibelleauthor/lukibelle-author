Sergeant Solkian Frashtakh

“It is Sergeant Solkian Frashtakh! Again! Greet me properly civilian!” shouted the officer.

Sergeant Frashtakh then rudely asked Gulaan for his identification documents and stiffened upon seeing that the civilian was carrying a dispatch stamp of authorization from Ambassador General Masim Tropart.

“So, the ambassador sends a disabled Llehstanzite civilian to help advance the education of our people!!” he smacked his thigh with his hand and shook his head as though he was thoroughly amused “Well better one like this one than a whole civilian!” he looked over at his men who agreed with his boorish humor and nodded.
