The Vicious & The Virile VII

When instinct awakens, humanity is no longer the dominant force.

A world where civilization is fragile—and instinct is eternal.

Where the structures meant to contain violence begin to fracture, and what lies beneath emerges without restraint.

Instinct, survival, and the collapse of control.

From bloodlines marked by primal inheritance to landscapes where nature reclaims dominance, each story reveals a chilling truth:

Power does not always evolve—it sometimes reverts.

In worlds where survival overrides morality and instinct reshapes identity, humanity is no longer the defining force—it is merely one among many.

When control collapses, what remains is not chaos—it is truth in its most primal form.

The Systems Within This Story

Synopsis

A collection of dark, visceral stories where survival overrides morality and power takes its most primal form.

Across fractured landscapes—urban, wild, and mythic—these narratives explore the collapse of control, the awakening of instinct, and the transformation of identity under extreme conditions.

Each story reveals a different face of the same truth:

When civilization falters, what emerges is not unfamiliar—it is what has always existed beneath.
