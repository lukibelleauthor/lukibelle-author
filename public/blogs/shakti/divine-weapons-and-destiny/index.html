Divine Weapons and Destiny

Power alone is not enough.

It must be focused.

Directed.

Chosen.

The Trident of Kumarun is not merely a weapon.

It is a threshold.

The Meaning of the Trident

It represents access.

To Juwala.

To origin.

To divine authority.

But not all who seek it wish to return.

Some wish to conquer.

Destiny and Worthiness

A divine weapon does not respond to strength alone.

It responds to alignment.

To intention.

To purpose.

To something deeper than desire.

The Illusion of Control

Those who seek to wield divine power often believe they control it.

But power at that level does not submit.

It reveals.

And in revealing—

it transforms the one who dares to claim it.
