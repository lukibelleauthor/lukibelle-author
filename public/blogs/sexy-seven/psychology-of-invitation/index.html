The Psychology of Invitation

Not all invitations are conscious.

Some are built slowly— through memory, fear, longing, and silence.

In these stories, the supernatural is not random.

It is precise.

It responds to something deeply embedded within each character.

The Inner Signal

Every protagonist carries an internal signal.

A frequency shaped by past experiences, emotional wounds, and unspoken desires.

The supernatural recognizes this signal.

And answers it.

The Illusion of Control

We believe we control what enters our lives.

But what if what enters us…

was already inside?

The line between internal and external begins to blur.

And once blurred—

it cannot be restored.
