When Longing Becomes Dangerous

Longing is not harmless.

It is not passive.

It is a force that moves toward fulfillment.

And sometimes—

it chooses paths we would never consciously take.

The Threshold

There is always a moment.

A quiet threshold.

Where desire shifts from thought…

to action.

In that moment, something irreversible begins.

The Price of Fulfillment

To have what one deeply desires is not always liberation.

Sometimes, it is transformation.

Sometimes, it is loss.

And sometimes—

it is both.

In The Sexy Seven Supernaturals, longing does not fade.

It evolves.

Into something that answers back.
