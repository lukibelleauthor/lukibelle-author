Mother Balaktaan (Jashmitha)

A warrior who chose exile over absolution—and became legend beneath the ocean.

Once a revered daughter and warrior, Jashmitha now exists in exile within the oceans, guarding a world that cost her everything—including her son.

She gave up her world to protect it—and became its unseen shield.

Archetype

The Exiled Guardian

Psychological Core

Unbreakable, self-reliant, and bound by honor, she carries grief without regret and duty without hesitation.

Central Conflict

Torn between her vow to protect the Western Continent and her final longing to see her son before the battle that may end her life.

Transformation Arc

Faced with an apocalyptic threat, she must decide whether to embrace ultimate sacrifice to secure the future she has spent centuries protecting.
