Guardian Zain

A warrior who mastered restraint—until violence demanded to be unleashed.

The eldest and most powerful of the Guardians, Zain is both protector and stabilizer of the Western Continent—balancing diplomacy against the beast within.

He kept the beast within chained—until the world gave him reason to release it.

Archetype

The Restrained Warlord

Psychological Core

Charismatic, calculating, and deeply disciplined, he suppresses his brutal nature through control, wit, and strategic thinking.

Central Conflict

Struggles to maintain order and restraint while confronting desires, rivalries, and instincts that threaten to break his control.

Transformation Arc

When forced to unleash his true nature, he must face the consequences of becoming what he has spent centuries trying to contain.
