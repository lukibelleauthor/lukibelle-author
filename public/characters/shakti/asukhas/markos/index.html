Markos

A mind that understands power—but cannot escape the fear beneath it.

A master of psychology and political influence, Markos survives through intellect and proximity to power—while hiding the fragility he fears will destroy him.

He mastered the minds of others—but remains captive to his own.

Archetype

The Shadow Advisor

Psychological Core

Insecure, manipulative, and deeply dependent, he masks vulnerability with intellect, control, and calculated influence.

Central Conflict

Struggles between maintaining his position of power and confronting the emotional dependency that defines his survival.

Transformation Arc

As the kingdom destabilizes, he must choose where his allegiance lies—revealing whether he is guided by fear or by strategy.
