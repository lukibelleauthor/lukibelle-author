Guardian Niv

A warrior driven by love lost—who cannot survive without the fire of revenge.

A fierce Guardian fueled by grief, Niv lives between two worlds—one of order under AlexanDRA, and one of chaos in his relentless pursuit of vengeance.

He lives for vengeance—but must decide who he is without it.

Archetype

The Vengeful Seeker

Psychological Core

Passionate, rebellious, and emotionally driven, he thrives on conflict and purpose born from loss.

Central Conflict

Caught between the structured peace of the Western Continent and his consuming need to avenge those he lost.

Transformation Arc

When offered freedom from his pain, he must choose between letting go of revenge—or losing the very force that defines him.
