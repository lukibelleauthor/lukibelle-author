Enya

A soul untouched by violence—forced into a world that demands it.

Raised in protection and longing for affection, Enya is a fragile yet intuitive presence navigating a reality she was never meant to endure.

She longed to be protected from the world—until the world demanded she face it.

Archetype

The Fragile Oracle

Psychological Core

Emotionally dependent, perceptive, and deeply fearful, she seeks comfort and validation while avoiding the brutality that defines her world.

Central Conflict

Caught between her need for protection and a growing awareness that survival may demand she become what she fears.

Transformation Arc

When war arrives, she is forced to confront violence directly—shattering her innocence and altering her destiny irrevocably.
