Guardian Luke

A man who chose peace—and must protect it from those who cannot live within it.

A devoted Guardian and family man, Luke upholds the stability of the Western Continent through communication, order, and unwavering belief in the system he serves.

He believed in the system that gave him peace—until that system was tested by those who refused it.

Archetype

The Dutiful Protector

Psychological Core

Grounded, loyal, and emotionally centered, he values peace, family, and structure above all else, rarely questioning the world he inhabits.

Central Conflict

Forced to cover for Guardian Niv’s rebellion, he struggles between loyalty to order and empathy for a pain he cannot fully understand.

Transformation Arc

As tensions rise, he must confront whether blind loyalty preserves peace—or enables its eventual collapse.
