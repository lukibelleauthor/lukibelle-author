Ahankara

Power that seeks not to return—but to destroy.

A being born of divine lineage yet consumed by rebellion, Ahankara does not seek restoration—he seeks domination.

Some do not seek redemption—they seek to rewrite existence itself.

Archetype

The Destroyer

Psychological Core

Driven by resentment and ambition, he rejects divine order and seeks to redefine power through control and annihilation.

Central Conflict

Locked in opposition to both divine authority and earthly power structures.

Transformation Arc

From exiled entity to a force that threatens the balance of all realms.
