# The Origin of the Asukhas

Before power was divided, it was unified.

Before exile, there was belonging.

---

The Asukhas were not always rulers of hidden worlds.

They were once disciples.

Students of divine knowledge within the sacred realm of Juwala.

---

## The First Fracture

The banishment of Kunjahl and Ahankara was not merely punishment.

It was a fracture in the structure of divinity itself.

---

What was once contained within the divine realm was released into the mortal world.

Not weakened.

But transformed.

---

## Power on Earth

On earth, the Asukhas evolved.

They adapted.

They learned to exist among humans—not as conquerors, but as unseen rulers.

---

Power became subtle.

Strategic.

Invisible.

---

## Legacy of Exile

Exile did not diminish them.

It defined them.

---

Because what is cast out does not disappear.

It reshapes itself.

And waits.

---

**The Asukhas are not a fallen lineage.
They are a transformed one.**
