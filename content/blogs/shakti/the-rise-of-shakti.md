something responds.

It responded to disruption.

To imbalance.

To violence.

---

And from that response—

something new emerged.

Shakti is not simply strength.

She is balance.

Creation.

Destruction.

Transformation.

---

She is what restores what has been broken—

and what redefines what cannot be restored.

Not control.

Not domination.

But evolution.
