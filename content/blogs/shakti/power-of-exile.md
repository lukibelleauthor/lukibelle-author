# The Power of Exile

Exile is often seen as loss.

But in truth—

it is transformation.

---

To be removed from origin is to be forced into becoming.

---

## Beyond Belonging

Kunjahl and Ahankara did not lose power when they were cast out.

They lost limitation.

---

Freed from divine structure, they entered a world where power could evolve unchecked.

---

## The Dual Nature of Exile

Exile creates two paths:

One seeks return.

The other seeks domination.

---

And in that divergence—

conflict becomes inevitable.

---

## Transformation Through Separation

To be separated from origin is to redefine identity.

To choose what one becomes.

---

Exile is not an ending.

It is a beginning disguised as loss.
