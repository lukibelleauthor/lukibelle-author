# The Psychology of Colonel Baquiste

Colonel Valstohl Baquiste is not merely a military figure—he is a study in contradiction.

Driven by discipline, shaped by war, and constrained by prejudice, his character reflects the internal conflict between authority and humanity.

## Pride and Identity

Baquiste’s identity is rooted in conquest and hierarchy. His prejudice is not incidental—it is systemic, inherited from the structures he serves.

## Transformation

What makes Baquiste compelling is not his strength, but his fracture. His journey forces him into unfamiliar terrain—both physically and psychologically.

This is where the story truly begins.
