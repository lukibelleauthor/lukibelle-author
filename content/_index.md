<h2 id="about">About the Author</h2>

<div class="author-section">

  <div class="about-text">
    <p class="about-intro">
      Luki Belle writes dark, character-driven fiction that explores power, prejudice, identity, and transformation.
    </p>
    <p>
      Storytelling has been a constant presence in her life since childhood, when she listened to stories shared by her grandparents, parents, sisters, and cousins—many drawn from diverse cultural traditions. Growing up across different parts of the world, Luki developed a deep appreciation for cultural diversity and a lasting belief that through love, pain, and grief, human connection can lead to profound and unexpected outcomes.
    </p>
    <p>
      From an early age, she was especially drawn to ghost stories and tales of the uncanny. This fascination evolved into a deeper interest in what it means to feel haunted—whether by memory, desire, or unseen forces, both invited and uninvited.
    </p>
    <p>
      In <em>The Sexy Seven Supernaturals</em>, Luki explores the intersection of the psychological and the paranormal, where human longing and subconscious distress give rise to forces beyond comprehension.
    </p>
    <p class="about-highlight">
      Some encounters are not accidents — they are invitations answered.
    </p>
    <p>
      Her early immersion in richly illustrated books and mythological traditions—particularly those of South Asia, Greece, and Rome—continues to influence her storytelling. These inspirations later guided the creation of her debut novel, <em>Shakti of the Illuminated Lotus</em>.
    </p>
    <p>
      Luki is drawn to worlds where the human and the supernatural coexist—where mystical forces amplify emotion, and where conflicted, tormented characters are pushed into moments of reckoning. This thematic tension is central to her work, including <em>The Vicious & The Virile VII</em>, where power, desire, and identity collide across fantastical realms.
    </p>

  </div>
