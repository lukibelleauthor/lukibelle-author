<h1>The Invitation</h1>

<p class="scene-subtitle">
Desire does not knock. It waits to be noticed.
</p>

  <img src="/images/S7-S1.png" alt="Scene 1 - The Photo and The Letter">

<p>
It was a glass picture frame, a photo of Sasha sitting in a stunning
wedding dress in a fully blossomed garden, against the backdrop of
a magnificent mansion. And though I had seen many pictures of her
during that evening, in exotic locations, this one stood out because
she looked quite happy. There were flowers in her hair and the sun’s
perfect angle upon her face was making her glow.


</p>

<p>
“My dear, you have broken my heart in so many pieces that I feel
like I live now without one, yet, feeling the pain of it being torn to shreds.
It is that pain with which I must now live for the rest of my life. How
could you betray me? When I gave my everything to you and loved you
like my own flesh and blood! My soul and harmony, I found nectar in
your essence and to think that you would do such a cruel deed to me
makes me want to tear my flesh so that I strip myself of pain forever, the
pain of watching you in bed with my very own brother! I damn you! I
damn you for the rest of your life!...."
</p>

<p>
Larissa should not have opened the letter.
But longing is a quiet persuader.
</p>
