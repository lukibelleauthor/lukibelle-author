<h1>Nature is Always Watching</h1>

<p class="scene-subtitle">
Never underestimate a weeping willow.
</p>

<img src="/images/S7-S6.png" alt="Scene 6 - The Old Willow Tree">

<p>
“Hello! I am Helen and I was really attracted to your home. 
I was jogging in the neighborhood and did not realize the brick lane would
lead to this property. Are you the owner of this gorgeous place?” 
she stood a few inches away from him on the steps of the porch.

</p>

<p>
The man shook his head.

</p>

<p>
“Can I meet the owner?” she asked in her innocent act.
She noticed the muddiness on the man’s overall and on his bare feet.

</p>

<p>
The man shook his head again.

</p>

<p>
“Who are you?” she asked.
</p>

<p>
The man took off his sunglasses exposing a pair of stunning green
eyes. He seemed to be younger than her.

“Caretaker” he replied in a hoarse voice with the pipe in his mouth
and with a smile.

“Oh! So you are the caretaker! Wow! Sir you have kept this place
looking really wonderful! I would really like to live here!” Helen
blurted out without thinking. 

It was unlike her to be so hasty in deal making.
</p>

<p>
At this, the man’s smile disappeared, he took the pipe out of his
mouth and pointed toward the property’s entrance as though telling
Helen to leave.
</p>
