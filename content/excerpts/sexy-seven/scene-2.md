<h1>Dare to Let Go</h1>

<p class="scene-subtitle">
Fear is only the body remembering its limits.
</p>

<img src="/images/S7-S2.png" alt="Scene 2 - The Strength of Feathers">

<p>
Vanessa looked to her left and saw that where the triangular
observation deck angled off, a woman and a man were standing there.

It was Lady Eliza and Sebastian Drake. He was looking out into the
skyline. 

Lady Eliza was standing close to him while looking towards
Vanessa, she had a smile on her face.
</p>

<p>
“It is time to fly my dear! To transform yourself, say Mashig Kena
Saltu Mataye Aasra Jameez Ebuta to return to your human form, think
the words Chagaal Nemsaruth Basyen Mentora Yassmi” 
Sebastian said and smiled warmly at Vanessa.
</p>

<p>
“Do not resist me...” he whispered “...this is only the beginning!”
</p>

<p>
Vanessa realized then—
some offers are not meant to be survived.
</p>
